# Product

## Register
product

## Users
AI-curious builders, researchers, founders, students, and journalists who want a quick, source-aware view of where AI capabilities, research activity, and market size are moving. They are comparing fields and benchmarks, often skimming before clicking through to original sources.

## Product Purpose
AI Progress Tracker is a read-only public dashboard for tracking AI progress across fields with curated benchmark charts, arXiv research-activity proxies, market-size/forecast data, and high-signal AI links. Success means users can quickly understand trends, compare fields, and trust the provenance and caveats behind the data.

## Brand Personality
Clear, analytical, restrained. The product should feel credible and calm, closer to a research dashboard than a hype page.

## Anti-references
Avoid hype-heavy AI SaaS marketing, ornamental gradients, glassy decorative surfaces, over-animated dashboards, and chartjunk that makes data harder to read. Do not make the interface feel like a trading terminal or a gamified leaderboard.

## Design Principles
- Data first: charts, values, caveats, and sources should be easier to scan than decoration.
- Earn trust: provenance, approximations, and uncertainty should be visible without overwhelming the page.
- Compare quickly: field and benchmark relationships should be legible at a glance.
- Preserve calm density: support rich information while keeping spacing, hierarchy, and controls orderly.
- Respect the system: use the existing restrained light/dark token system and simple component vocabulary.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Aim for WCAG AA contrast and keyboard accessibility. Dark mode must remain first-class. Interactions should work without motion, color alone should not carry meaning, and charts should keep labels/tooltips readable at common desktop and mobile sizes.
